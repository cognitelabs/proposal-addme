Explanation of Services

To help get in sync about how we understand the work we’ll do, we offer the following explanation of our services.

Information Architecture

Determine site & business development goals

Create personas & buyer questions

For inbound marketing to be truly effective, you have to know who you're talking to. We'll take what you already know, bring in some new insights, and combine them into several buyer personas. These fictitious people represent an ideal client for your company and we'll use them as the basis for a content strategy.

Outline sitemap

Keyword plan

We'll coordinate with your team to create campaigns that work for your personas. We'll research relevant keywords to help direct your content production efforts, then optimize posts upon completion. Content should be written for humans, not search engines, but phrasing & document structure can go a long way with a little optimization.

Determine calls to action for site pages

Calls to action move people through your website through content offers and lead magnets. This is how you get more customers. We'll make sure there are opportunities for engagement on every page.

Content Strategy

Content audit & blog optimization

We'll review the current content at your site and identify opportunities to enhance it for lead generation, including calls to action and interative mailing list subscriptions

Set up email marketing

We'll set up an email marketing platform that facilitates personalized marketing automation, so that you can producing engaging interactions with minimal effort.

Create content offers

New offers create new leads, so we'll need to continually create new content that your customers will want to read. We'll coordinate with your team to produce one modest new offer each month. Your team will provide the information & body of knowledge and we'll provide the expertise in formatting, layout, and delivery.

Competitive analysis & benchmarking

We'll take a look at 'best-in-class' competitors in your industry, learn from what they're doing well, and exploit weaknesses in their strategies.

Web Design

Design visual layouts for site pages (mobile-first)

We'll create visually-appealing themes and layouts for the informational pages on your site.

Design visual layouts for blog

We'll create visually-appealing themes and layouts for your blog, with an eye towards lead generation.

Create interactive prototype of the site

Before we launch your site, we'll create an interactive prototype, so that there is no ambiguity about the look and feel of the site before it launches.

Web Development

Code & deploy website

Once we've settled on a design, we'll code & deploy your website.

Reporting

Monthly reporting

We'll provide ongoing strategy & monthly reporting of our progress, so you can feel confident that things are moving forward. We'll spnd time helping you to analyze existing results and strategizing for the next step.

Implement Google Analytics & Funnel Tracking

Implement Google Analytics, create conversion goals and tracking URLs for your landing pages, and design a dashboard that reflects your marketing goals.
